Research Statement

Calcific Aortic Valve Stenosis results in the calcification of heart valves which obstructs blood flow in affected patients. Previous research has demonstrated that hemodynamic changes are associated with aortic valve stiffening; the association between non-uniform calcification and energy dissipation remains under investigation. My future research aims to model this condition and demonstrate what, if any, causal relationship exists between leaflet stiffness and an increased energy dissipation in the left ventricle. These findings, if significant, could explain the onset of symptoms associated with Calcific Aortic Valve Stenosis, such as exercise intolerance and fatigue.
